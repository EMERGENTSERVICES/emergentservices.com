About Us

Who We Are

A team of licensed electricians committed to keeping your lights on and your property safe — around the clock.

Our Story

Built on Urgency.
Driven by Quality.

Emergent Services was founded on a simple idea: when people have an electrical emergency, they need help now — not tomorrow morning, not after a callback queue. That urgency is in our name and in every job we take on.

We're a full-service electrical company offering everything from emergency repairs to complete new installations. Our team of licensed, insured electricians brings professionalism, speed, and attention to detail to every project — whether it's a residential outlet repair or a large-scale commercial buildout.

We don't cut corners. We don't overcharge. And when you call us at 2 AM because something's wrong, we pick up the phone.
